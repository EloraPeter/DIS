Privacy Policy

This privacy statement explains what personal data Data Insights Solutions Ltd. collects from you and how we use that data.

Last updated on August 6, 2024.

Who We Are

We are Data Insights Solutions Ltd. (DIS Ltd.), a provider of data analytics services headquartered in Delta State, Nigeria. As you read this document, you are currently visiting our website, which is a digital face of our company. We are committed to providing a great experience while you are using our website, sharing relevant materials, clarifying any questions, and ensuring smooth functioning of the site. This is only possible with your assistance in sharing personal data. We take your privacy very seriously and have developed this privacy policy to detail how we handle your personal data.

What Personal Data We Collect

DIS Ltd. collects data to provide you with feedback regarding our services. You provide some of this data directly, such as when you fill in contact forms or other forms on our website. We also collect data using technologies like cookies, and receive error reports or usage data from software running on your device.

We obtain data from third parties as well. These sources may vary, but currently include:

  • Service providers that help us determine your location based on your IP address.
  • Partners with whom we offer co-branded services or engage in joint marketing activities.
  • Publicly available sources such as government databases or other public domain data.

You have choices about the data we collect. If you decline to provide necessary data, you may not be able to use certain services or receive feedback.

The data we collect includes:

  • Name and Contact Data: First and last name, email address, phone number, company name.
  • Demographic Data: Country, preferred language.
  • Website Data: Interaction patterns, visitor behavior, configuration data (e.g., IP address), error reports, and performance data.
  • Troubleshooting and Help Data: Data related to troubleshooting, including contact data, communication content, system and registry data.

We do not knowingly collect data from children or private individuals. Parents can manage consent and request deletion of their children's data. We may also collect data from third parties with your consent or from publicly available sources.

Why We Collect Personal Data

DIS Ltd. collects data for the following purposes:

  • Sending communications, including promotional materials.
  • Advertising and analyzing website visitor statistics.
  • Technical troubleshooting and improving user experience.

We do not rent or trade email lists. We use technological and procedural safeguards to protect your data and offer choices about receiving promotional communications.

Why We Share Personal Data

We share your data with our team that handles your requests. All parties involved must adhere to our data privacy and security requirements. We may access, transfer, disclose, or preserve personal data when necessary to comply with laws, protect clients, or enforce our terms.

Some website pages may link to third-party products with different privacy practices. Your data is governed by their policies if shared with them.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • View, amend, or delete the personal information we hold about you.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.
  • Recall your consent for data processing at any time.

Requests can be sent to our data protection officer at data-protection-officer@datainsightssolutions.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Cookies & Similar Technologies

DIS Ltd. uses cookies and similar technologies to provide and improve our services. Cookies help us detect fraud, store information, and gather website statistics.

You can control cookies through your browser settings and may also encounter other similar technologies like Local Shared Objects.

Notice to End Users

DIS Ltd. delivers services to organizations. If your organization administers your use of our website, please direct privacy inquiries to them. DIS Ltd. is not responsible for the privacy practices of our clients.

How We Secure Your Data

We use various security technologies to protect your data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. Personal data is stored in secure databases with restricted access.

Data Retention

We retain personal data as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, or enforce agreements. Retention periods vary depending on data sensitivity and legal requirements.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We update this privacy statement to reflect changes in practices or feedback. Changes will be posted with a revised date. Material changes will be communicated through notices or notifications.

How to Contact Us

If you have privacy concerns or questions, please email our Data Protection Officer at data-protection-officer@datainsightssolutions.com. We will respond within 30 days.
